HFS Analyzer

Automated tonic-clonic decomposition of hemifacial spasm severity from smartphone video

All processing runs locally — no data leaves your device

Usage Notice

  • This tool is for research/support use and is not a standalone diagnostic device.
  • Use clear frontal face video with stable framing and adequate lighting.
  • Always interpret outputs together with clinical findings and physician judgment.
  • Do not include personally identifiable information in filenames or exported data when sharing.
